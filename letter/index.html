The Letter

Dear Senators and Representatives:

Please strike out the language in the Government Appropriations bill that would block the FCC from enforcing its net neutrality rules. It is unacceptable to use a budget bill to circumvent the FCC's open rulemaking process that millions of citizens participated in.

The American people have called for strong net neutrality rules. Congress should let the FCC do its job, and not engage in unproductive partisan brinksmanship.

Thank you,
[Your name here]
