This is a battle for the Internet's future.

The FCC is planning to gut rules that stop ISPs from messing with what we see, do, and say online. John Oliver made a great video about it—which everyone needs to see!—but he points people to the FCC's site, which makes commenting difficult even when it's working properly (and it crashes all the time). So watch & share the John Oliver video, but share this page, not the Youtube link!
